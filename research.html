American Legislative Politics and Revolving-Door Laws

Details

Research Assistant with Prof. Fred Gui, Louisiana State University
July 2026 – Present
Keywords: American legislative politics; revolving-door laws; political institutions; legislative behavior

I assist with a research project examining how U.S. revolving-door laws affect legislators and legislative behavior. My work focuses on collecting, organizing, and cleaning data related to state-level legal and political institutions.

Main Contributions:
• Collected and cleaned data on U.S. revolving-door laws and related institutional rules.
• Organized state-level legal and political information for empirical analysis.
• Developed a stronger research interest in the relationship between institutional design and legislative behavior.
